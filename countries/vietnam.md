# Vietnam — Nomad Notes

*Compiled from community digests.*

## Da Nang

Recommended as a beginner-friendly nomad destination, often suggested as an alternative to Bangkok with better vibe and climate. Paired with nearby Hoi An for variety.

### Neighborhoods

East of the airport / Han River area near the beach is the most recommended zone for nomads — walkable to cafes and coworking, close to the coast.

### Cafes & Coworking

Login Cafe noted as an active nomad morning spot.

## Hoi An

Frequently mentioned alongside Da Nang as part of the central Vietnam corridor. Regarded as having a better atmosphere than Bangkok for those who find the Thai capital overwhelming.

### Cafes & Coworking

Hub Hoi An coworking praised (as of April 2026).

## Visa & Tax

At immigration after visa runs, stick to "tourism" as purpose of visit — officers rarely probe further. Having an onward ticket ready is advisable (Onwardify is one tool for generating these). Be aware of tax residency implications of extended stays.

Land border crossings reported as problematic for Indian nationals specifically — airport entries are smoother.

Responsive same-day visa agencies exist (~$60) — ask in community channels for current recommendations.

## Practical

### Money Transfers

Glintz app recommended for transferring money into VND via Napas 247 (as of April 2026). Community comparison tools exist for evaluating transfer rates.
