# Vietnam — Nomad Notes

*Compiled from community digests.*

## Da Nang

Recommended as a beginner-friendly nomad destination, often suggested as an alternative to Bangkok with better vibe and climate. Paired with nearby Hoi An for variety.

## Hoi An

Frequently mentioned alongside Da Nang as part of the central Vietnam corridor. Regarded as having a better atmosphere than Bangkok for those who find the Thai capital overwhelming.
