Soverents [sove.re] is a commons for “sovereign constituents” aka high agency people interested in human flourishing. In the short history since Zuzalu, “pop up villages” such as Edge City, Zuitzerland and The Mu and many others have been building laboratories for [[Governance Technology]] and [[Social Technology]] to pave the way for future ways of living.

SOVERENTS FORUM

The Soverents Forum (forum.sove.re) is being hosted as an open and easy to access tool for discussion and collaboration for all people and projects related to this theme of actualized utopia. It fills a gap between long form content like blogs and the highly ephemeral medium of instant messaging.
