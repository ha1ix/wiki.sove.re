# Overview

Vitalik Buterin is the founder of [Ethereum](/technology/software/ethereum) and the initiator of [Zuzalu](/network-societies/pop-ups/zuzalu) (Montenegro, 2023). His writing is the primary intellectual thread connecting this wiki's major topics: network states, pop-up cities, d/acc, programmable cryptography, and decentralized governance.

# Writings relevant to this wiki

- **[Crypto Cities](/network-societies/crypto-cities-vb)** (2021) — explores blockchain integration into local governance; precursor to Zuzalu thinking
- **[Network States review](https://vitalik.eth.limo/general/2022/07/13/networkstates.html)** (2022) — response to Balaji Srinivasan's book
- **[Why I Built Zuzalu](/network-societies/pop-ups/zuzalu/why-vb)** (2023) — first-hand account of Zuzalu's design and lessons
- **[d/acc](/philosophy/dacc)** (2023) — Defensive Accelerationism essay
- **[Futures of Ethereum](https://vitalik.eth.limo/general/2024/10/29/futures6.html)** (2024) — includes overview of "God Protocols" and programmable cryptography

His blog is at [vitalik.eth.limo](https://vitalik.eth.limo).
