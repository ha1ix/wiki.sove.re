# Overview

E. Glen Weyl is an economist and co-author of two works directly relevant to this wiki's themes. He co-authored the 2018 paper *Liberal Radicalism* with [Vitalik Buterin](/people/vitalik-buterin) and ZoÃ« Hitzig, which introduced [Quadratic Funding](/technology/software/quadratic-funding). He also co-authored *Plurality: The Future of Collaborative Technology and Democracy* with Audrey Tang, the basis of the [Pluralism](/philosophy/pluralism) page.

He founded [RadicalxChange](https://radicalxchange.org), a foundation exploring new mechanisms for democratic coordination and market design.
