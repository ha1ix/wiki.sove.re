# Overview

Janine Leger co-organized the original [Zuzalu](/network-societies/pop-ups/zuzalu) (Montenegro, 2023) and subsequently co-founded [Edge City](/network-societies/pop-ups/edge-city) with [Timour Kosters](/people/timour-kosters), which became the primary continuing series of pop-up villages in the Zuzalu lineage.
